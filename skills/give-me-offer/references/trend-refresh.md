# Trend Refresh

Use this when the user asks about hot roles, current hiring trends, AI-related job directions, or when role guidance may have changed recently.

## When to Browse

Browse when:

- user asks for current, latest, trending, hot, 2026, or market advice
- target role is new or fast-changing, such as AI agent, AIGC operations, multimodal AI, data governance, AI product, or low-code automation
- advice may affect expensive decisions, such as changing career direction

Prefer recent job posts, official reports, credible labor-market sources, and company career pages.

## Refresh Procedure

1. Collect 5-10 recent JD samples or credible summaries for the target region.
2. Extract repeated hard skills, tools, tasks, and seniority signals.
3. Separate long-term capabilities from short-term buzzwords.
4. Update resume keywords and portfolio suggestions.
5. Warn if a role is too senior, too saturated, or poorly matched.
