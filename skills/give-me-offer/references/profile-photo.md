# Profile Photo Guidance

Use this when the user asks for ID photos, resume photos, professional headshots, LinkedIn photos, Chinese resume photos, US-style headshots, or AI-generated photo prompts.

## First Decision

Ask or infer target country/region, industry, resume language, and platform.

In many China-facing resumes, a professional photo may be expected. In US-style resumes and many international/foreign-company contexts, avoid putting a photo in the resume unless requested by the platform or industry norm.

## Style Options

- **Chinese campus recruitment**: clean ID photo, white/blue/gray background, neat shirt or blazer, natural lighting.
- **Professional business**: head-and-shoulders, neutral background, blazer/shirt, confident expression.
- **US-style headshot**: natural professional portrait, no ID-photo rigidity, simple background, friendly expression.
- **Clean studio portrait**: polished lighting, soft but realistic skin, modern studio look, restrained retouching.
- **Creative/operations/media**: slightly warmer, approachable, still professional.

## Prompt Template

```text
Generate a realistic professional headshot for a [candidate type] applying to [industry/role].
Style: [Chinese ID photo / US professional headshot / clean studio portrait].
Composition: head and shoulders, facing camera, natural confident expression.
Clothing: [white shirt / navy blazer / simple business casual].
Background: [white / light gray / blue / soft studio].
Lighting: soft even professional lighting.
Constraints: realistic, no heavy beauty filter, no exaggerated retouching, no logos, no text, no accessories that distract.
```
